Credits

Hidden Depths has been put together with the help of teachers, reserchers and specialists. The content in these lesson plans, video and images has drawn on the work of original research as well as open source and creative commons content. We would like to say a big thankyou to everyone involved and acknowledge the assets used in the creation of this project.

Images audio, and 3D assets

The video short and still images in the Hidden Depths project were made with original drawings, photographs, audio, modelling and animation but would not have been possible without the generousity of the creative commons community.

A big thankyou to all those providing creative commons material including: Felix Blume, keweldog, mullumbimby, ali-k, blaukreuz, ninafoletti, roubignolle (audio) and Evgeni Tcher (milkyway image).

Images produced for Ohalo (all), KNM-ER 1808 (healthy-not healthy), Shanidar 1 (healthy) and Romito 2 (skeleton) were produced using assets from Cadnav.com.

The header image for lesson three (two wolves) used an asset by 3Dhaupt on blendswap.org.

Other images used in the website are credited directly.

Images in the hidden depths website are provided under CC-BY-NC with the exception of those including original non-creative commons imagery (e.g. photographs by institutions and individuals not listed above) where original copyright rights remain.

3D modelling in this project was carried out with OpenSource software Blender.org
